App 1

Foodiez Loco

Year 1 Sem 2

Foodiez Loco

Overview

This a assignment that I had to for one of my core module in Year 1 Semester 2 where I have to design and develop a responsive website for a company revamp (real or fictional) or a fictional company site of my own. The main goal of doing this assignment is for us students to build skills in web development and design.

I decided to go with doing a website based on food and deserts as I am a foodie myself which is also why I named the website Foodie Loco.



Role

Web Developer

Wireframing, Interaction, Coding, Visual Design, Prototyping & Testing

07 Nov 2022 - 04 Dec 2022

Background

This project is about a website that shows food that is sold in a actual restaurant, these food that is sold is commonly eaten food in social media such as TikTok and Instagram. Customers can chose their favorite food from the menu's called "Meals" and "Desserts" and order it in the contact page by calling the restaurant and telling them their order and the restaurant will send them to their house within 5 to 10 minutes. This is a business I created because I love food and the varieties of food that exist around the world and also viral as they are very tasty and aesthetically nice to take pictures and post in social media. The main target audience or users are social influencers to promote the website and the restaurant and people who enjoys eating variety of food but the restaurant is open for all ages - young to old to enjoy their cravings at.

I got inspired by many food vlogs and YouTube videos I watch to make this website and since I love anime too, I added a little snippet of it in my home page. I briefly did a About us page too so that customers briefly can know what our company is about and how it got created in the first place. Why the customers can't order from the website? To me I believe that's a hassle, as we need do a lot procedures, but with a contact page and simple call to the company, the food will arrive in minutes and have the customers enjoy the food happily with a great service done.

Design Process

This website is mainly for people who loves food and mostly the food vloggers around the world.

This website achieves the purpose of sharing our very own way of selling food by using variety ingredients and seasonings from the original recipe, so that customers will get astonished from the very own creative taste and making our business go viral and have the these business to grow and be a multi-billionaire company.

This website is just the beginning of the journey for this business, where our website is viewed and shared through social media and having great reviews after the service is done, in this way it will be easier to have the goal to be achieved as nothing social medias and websites and viral platforms used by many people around the world.

My website creation and solving of the problem is mainly based on following the wireframes created so that I will have the basic idea of what content should be implemented , what the layouts and pages be included inside.

Understanding The Problem

Nowadays most of the food is ordered online and less eating in restaurants but one thing that bothers most users is how long will the delivery to deliver, the amount of details to be filled for the checkout and order details. For me personally, I feel like feel that feeling in the details and the whole process of food ordering is troublesome and a waste of time which why this website will bring the solution to these problems.

These are user story scenarios for when to use the website:

  • User Story #1:
  • As a user using this website, I want to have the most preferred food that I crave at the particular moment, so that I can have my cravings to satisficed.


  • User Story #2:
  • As a user who do food vlogs in social media, I want to have food that aesthetically looking and also with a good amount of portion of food, so that not only it can satisfy my hunger but also have my social media updated and having my followers to see the company's amazing food design and view and also how the food taste like.


  • User Story #3:
  • As a user who have a tired day at work and also ended late from work, I want to have food that can be delivered fast and quickly without having to do the whole ordering process but with one single call, so that I can have the food on time and also just do a simple process to order the food and having the great service with a great review given in the end.

Wireframing The Website

The website mainly follows the layout of the wireframe as said earlier. Wireframes are good start when starting a project especially when comes to making a website. I used Adobe XD to create the wireframes for the website so that is is clearer and easier to view. I made these wireframes based on the problems identified as above, I planned the layout by coming with these solutions:

  • Not providing checkout page but providing the contact page instead, as it better to make a simple phone call than doing the whole checkout process which make take some time.


  • Providing images and descriptions for the menu, so that users can view what they would be ordering.


  • Inserting interactions on buttons and also a header video for a interactive experience for the users and not let the users have a boring time using the website.

I planned the basic look of the website to show the module teacher before making the actual website so that I can know what would the required changes I need to do. This involved the overall layout and content that would be included in the website. I did wireframes for both the mobile and desktop versions.



Existing Features

Feature 1 > Navigation Bar - Is to allow users to direct them to different pages of the website by clicking on the navigation bar buttons given.

Feature 2 > Header Video & Button > User can view the interactive video in the header and also click on the button provided to click for the user to direct them to a external link of a different website called Epicurious which is also a online food blog website, the user can have a interactive with the button as it has a interactive hover effect when clicked.

Feature 3 > About Us Page button > Same as the header session, it has a interactive hover effect when clicked which link to the website called Meals to check the food in that particular website.

Feature 4 > Feature Cards & Button > Is to allow user to view Meals and Deserts page with interest and use the button that's also have a interactive effect when hovered provided to go to the Meals and Deserts page.

Feature 5 > Footer Subscribe Form > Users can type in their email inside to subscribe to the website and when correct email is not given they will have a pop up alert that shows that a correct email link should be given.

Feature 6 > Meals/Desert Page Order Buttons > Users can go both these pages and view the food provided and click on the interactive button that changes colors when hovered over and click the button to go to the the contact page to order the food.

Feature 7 > Contact Form, Button, Map > Users can go to these page and fill up the form and also click submit with a hover effect that changes color of the button. The map will allow user to move around the locations and have user to see other places.

Feature 8 > Sign Up Page & Buttons > Users can sign up to these page and the button also have a hover effect that changes color when hovered and the Log In button can have users to go to the home page of the website.

Feature 9 > Logo Button > Users can press the logo button can have users to go to the homepage of the website.

Feature 10 > Navigation Responsive > When the navigation is on moble view it will change to a 3 line menu bar where when user press it can have a animation shown and also a dropdown menu with the different pages provided.

TikTok Infographic Chart

Year 1 Sem 1

Tiktok Infographic

Overview

This is an assignment that I had to do for one of the core module in Year 1 Semester 1 where I have to create a Infographic Chart to promote the mobile app which I chosen was TikTok from my previous assignments.

I decided to do TikTok as my app because I like the theme and the colors they use and the purpose of the app. I was able to easily change the colors to my own version with my own idea by the theme of the app.

Role

Graphic Designer

Wireframing/Sketching, Adobe Illustrator

14 July 2022 - 8 Aug 2022

Background

This project is about making Infographic Charts for an mobile app. Infographic chart is a type of visual representation of facts, events or numbers. It has three major components: the contents, the visuals and the presentation. It is a good mixture of text and various sorts of graphics to convey the overwhelming information into an engaging and easy to understand content.

This assignment helps in showing our skill in designing, planning, sketching and also in using the various tools in Adobe Illustrator. The theme of the infographic chart matters too and also the concept behind it.

I chose TikTok as my app as it is entertaining and can decide on different themes easily. I was able to sketch out the ideas I want easily as I use the app daily enough too know the different features of the app. It was fun in doing the assignment and also getting know more doing a proper and effective infographic chart.

Design Process

This infographic chart was pretty simple to be maid but required a lot planning in the placement of different sections of the infographic chart. As for my infographic chart, it consists of the different features and also the limits when using the app. All information is accurate as I got it from Google itself.

I had to chose the theme that I be going for the infographic chart which I chose to a more vibrant and colorful theme from that of the original TikTok app which is mostly darker theme. Afterwards, I had to sketch the wireframe of the infographic chart in how roughly the chart is going to look like in the end. I was able to get a brief idea on how I wanted it to look like from the research done. Following that, we should use a artboard in Adobe Illustrator and design the sketches done to a actual infographic chart which as shown as above.

Overall, it was a easy design process but the sketching and planning process took longer in deciding the placement of texts, images and sections of the infographic chart but I was able to get a good result in the end with effective planning.

Wireframe/Sketching

This is the process of planning and wireframing for the infographic chart which shows the various sketches done in what should be included inside the chart.

Different colors and texts were used to show the overall look and feel of the chart and also in what is included inside the chart. Some of what should be included inside the chart was taken from Google where shows what TikTok has that the users doesn't know about and also maybe adding in interesting facts about the app inside the chart.

This was a 5 week project where we needed the consult the teacher every week and showing how our progress is for our chart where he was able to give positive comments for my idea and also give feedbacks if needed any changes to make the chart look better. I was able to take in the feedbacks to make the chart better for a good result in the end of the project.

Adobe Illustrator

I used this software in making the infographic chart where I was able to learn about a lot of tools in making the chart look better. I also made sure to follow the feedbacks given my teacher in doing the chart properly and also for it to look more appealing.

I used the trace tool mostly in making the visual looks to show the different functions of the app and also some for deco purposes. I also watched YouTube Videos, in making the chart more appealing by adding more texts or deco if needed. I also made sure to put in the right amount of spaced between the texts and the traced images to not make it look squeezy in the end.

Overall, I was able to get the result I was satisfied with and also in making the required changes as told by teacher during the weekly checkpoints so that the chart looks better in visual sense.

Results & Takeaways

After making the infographic chart, I was able to achieve the information relevant to the app and also getting the look I want from the amount of planning done for the chart. After submitting this chart, I was able to do a presentation and get a good feedback from the teacher and in receiving a A for this assignment which was what I was aiming for. The checkpoint meetings and the planning done was really effective and paid off the hardwork in getting a good result in the end.

Some key takeaways I got from this project are:

- Adding more space so it would less crowded and more spacious in between the texts and the traced images.

- Adding more information than two of them so viewers can know more about the app and be interested in it.

- Maybe add less flashy colors so that it does not look less appealing to the viewers.
